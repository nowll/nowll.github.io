    Arm Exercise Program

    1. Bicep Curls

    Instructions:

    • Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, holding a dumbbell in each hand with palms facing forward.
    • Keep your elbows close to your torso and curl the weights while contracting your biceps.
    • Continue to raise the weights until your biceps are fully contracted and the dumbbells are at shoulder level.
    • Slowly lower the dumbbells back to the starting position.

    Reps: 3 sets of 12-15 reps

    2. Tricep Dips

    Instructions:

    • Position your hands shoulder-width apart on a secured bench or stable chair. Ensure the bench or chair is stable and won't tip over during the exercise.
    • Sit on the edge of the bench and slide your buttocks off the front of the bench with your legs extended out in front of you. Keep your feet together and your heels on the ground. For an easier variation, keep your knees bent with your feet flat on the floor.
    • Grip the edge of the bench with your hands, keeping your fingers pointed forward or slightly outward. Your arms should be fully extended, supporting your weight.
    • Slowly lower your body by bending your elbows until they form a 90-degree angle. Keep your back close to the bench and your elbows pointing straight back. Your shoulders should stay down and away from your ears.

    Reps: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

    3. Hammer Curls

    Instructions:

    • Stand up with your torso upright and a dumbbell in each hand being held at arm's length.
    • Keep your elbows close to your torso and rotate the palms of your hands so that they are facing your torso.
    • While holding the upper arm stationary, curl the weights while contracting your biceps as you breathe out.
    • Continue to raise the weights until your biceps are fully contracted and the dumbbells are at shoulder level.
    • Slowly lower the dumbbells back to the starting position.

    Reps: 3 sets of 12-15 reps

    4. Overhead Tricep Extension

    Instructions:

    • Stand with feet hip-width apart and hold a dumbbell with both hands, extending it over your head.
    • Keep your elbows close to your head and lower the weight behind your head by bending your elbows.
    • Raise the dumbbell back to the starting position by straightening your arms.

    Reps: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
