    Leg Exercise

    A leg exercise program is designed to strengthen and tone the muscles of the lower body through targeted resistance training. It typically includes exercises such as squats, lunges, leg presses, and calf raises, with specific sets, repetitions, and rest periods tailored to individual fitness levels. This program helps improve leg strength, enhance stability, and support overall lower body health and fitness goals.

    Flexibility Exercise

    Flexibility exercise programs aim to enhance the range of motion in muscles and joints. These programs typically include stretching, yoga, and Pilates, focusing on lengthening muscles and improving joint mobility.
