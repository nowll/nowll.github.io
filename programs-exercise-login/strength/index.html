    Strength Exercise Program

    1. Deadlifts

    Instructions:

    • Stand with feet hip-width apart, toes pointing forward.
    • Keep your back straight, bend at your hips and knees to lower the barbell.
    • Grab the barbell with an overhand grip slightly wider than shoulder-width.
    • Keep your chest up, pull the barbell up, extending your hips and knees until standing tall.
    • Lower the barbell back down with control, keeping it close to your body.

    Reps: 3 sets of 8-10 reps

    2. Bench Press

    Instructions:

    • Lie on a bench with your feet flat on the floor.
    • Grip the barbell slightly wider than shoulder-width apart.
    • Push the barbell back up to the starting position, extending your arms fully.
    • Lock out your elbows without fully extending them at the top, to squeez your chest muscles.
    • Lower the barbell back to your mid-chest in a controlled manner.
    • Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

    Reps: 3 sets of 8-12 reps

    3. Overhead Squat

    Instructions:

    • Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, holding a barbell overhead with arms extended.
    • Lower into a squat position, keeping the barbell stable overhead.
    • Keep your chest up and back straight as you squat down until thighs are parallel to the floor.
    • Push through your heels to stand back up to the starting position.

    Reps: 3 sets of 8-10 reps

    4. Pull-Ups

    Instructions:

    • Grab a pull-up bar with an overhand grip, hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart.
    • Hang with arms fully extended.
    • Pull your body up until your chin passes the bar.
    • Lower yourself back down with control.

    Reps: 3 sets of 6-10 reps
