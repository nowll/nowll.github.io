    Flexibility Exercise Program

    1. Seated Forward Bend

    Instructions:

    • Sit on the floor with your legs extended in front of you.
    • Inhale deeply and lengthen your spine.
    • Exhale as you hinge at your hips, reaching forward towards your toes.
    • Keep your back straight and avoid rounding your spine.
    • Feel the stretch in your hamstrings and lower back.
    • Hold the stretch for 45-60 seconds while breathing deeply.
    • Release and repeat 3-4 times.

    2. Standing Quadriceps Stretch

    Instructions:

    • Stand tall with feet hip-width apart.
    • Bring one heel towards your buttock.
    • Keep your knees close together and gently push your hip forward.
    • Hold the stretch for 20-30 seconds, feeling a stretch in the front of your thigh.
    • Switch sides and repeat 2-3 times per leg.

    3. Child's Pose

    Instructions:

    • Kneel on the floor, touching your big toes together and sitting on your heels.
    • Slowly lower your chest towards your thighs and extend your arms forward.
    • Rest your forehead on the floor or a cushion and relax your entire body.
    • Feel the stretch along your spine, shoulders, and hips.
    • Hold the stretch for 45-60 seconds while taking deep breaths.
    • Repeat 3-4 times, focusing on relaxation and deepening the stretch.

    4. Standing Side Stretch

    Instructions:

    • Stand with feet hip-width apart and arms extended overhead.
    • Grab one wrist with the opposite hand and gently pull to one side.
    • Keep both feet firmly planted on the ground.
    • Hold the stretch for 20-30 seconds, feeling a stretch along your side body.
    • Switch sides and repeat 2-3 times per side.
