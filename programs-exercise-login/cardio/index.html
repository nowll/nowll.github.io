    Cardio Exercise Program

    1. Jogging

    Instructions:

    • Start with a warm-up walk for 5-10 minutes.
    • Gradually increase your pace to a comfortable jogging speed.
    • Focus on maintaining a steady rhythm and good posture.
    • Jog for 20-30 minutes, depending on your fitness level.
    • Cool down with a slow walk and stretch your muscles.

    Duration: 20-30 minutes

    2. Jumping Rope

    Instructions:

    • Hold the jump rope handles with palms facing forward.
    • Jump over the rope with both feet, landing softly on the balls of your feet.
    • Keep your elbows close to your body and wrists relaxed.
    • Start with 1-2 minutes of jumping, rest for 30 seconds, and repeat.
    • Gradually increase your jumping time as you build endurance.

    Duration: 10-20 minutes

    3. Cycling

    Instructions:

    • Adjust your bike seat to a comfortable height and check tire pressure.
    • Start with a 5-minute warm-up at an easy pace.
    • Pedal at a moderate intensity for 20-30 minutes.
    • Vary your speed and resistance to challenge yourself.
    • Cool down with 5-10 minutes of easy cycling and stretch your legs.

    Duration: 20-30 minutes

    4. High Knees

    Instructions:

    • Stand with feet hip-width apart.
    • Run in place, bringing your knees up towards your chest as high as possible.
    • Use your arms to help maintain momentum.
    • Perform for 1 minute, rest for 30 seconds, and repeat.
    • Increase your speed and height of knees as you get more comfortable.

    Duration: 5-10 minutes
